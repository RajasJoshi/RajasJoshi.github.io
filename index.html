Research

Simultaneous Navigator for Autonomous Identification and Localization Robot

R. Joshi, D. Bhaiya, A. Purkayastha, S. Patil, and A. Deshpande (2021).

This paper presents a robot system which is named SNAIL Bot. It uses a 2D 360-degree LiDAR and IMU to localize itself with respect to the environment. The robot is also equipped with a 2-DOF camera to detect ArUco markers for loop closures. The robot is able to generate a map of the environment and localize itself with respect to the map.

2021 IEEE Region 10 Symposium (TENSYMP), pp. 1-6, DOI: 10.1109/TENSYMP52854.2021.9550901
